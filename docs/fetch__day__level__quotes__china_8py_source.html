41 class DayLevelQuoteUpdaterTushare:
42  """
43  Fetch day level data of list of symbols given a date range.
44  Downloaded files should be saved to $PROGRAM_DATA_ROOT/day_quotes/china/%s.csv % symbol
45  $symbol.csv: contains day level data with header:
46  [date,open,high,close,low,volume,price_change,p_change,ma5,ma10,ma20,v_ma5,v_ma10,v_ma20,turnover,name,symbol]
47  where:
48  date: date in format YYYY-MM-DD
49  open: stock price at 09:30AM YYYY-MM-DD
50  high: highest price among all prices of YYYY-MM-DD
51  close: stock price at 15:00PM YYYY-MM-DD
52  low: lowest price among all prices of YYYY-MM-DD
53  volume: traded shares / 100
54  price_change: close - prev_close
55  p_change: (close - prev_close) / prev_close * 100
56  ma5: moving average of price, with window size = 5
57  ma10: moving average of price, with window size = 10
58  ma20: moving average of price, with window size = 20
59  v_ma5: moving average of volume, with window size = 5
60  v_ma10: moving average of volume, with window size = 10
61  v_ma20: moving average of volume, with window size = 20
62  turnover: volume / outstanding shares * 100
63  name: short company name
64  symbol: company symbol
65  """
